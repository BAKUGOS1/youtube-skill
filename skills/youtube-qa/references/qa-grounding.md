# Q&A grounding rules

## Answerable

Answer when one or more passages directly support the response. Cite the closest timestamp and include surrounding context when the sentence depends on a prior reference.

## Partially answerable

Answer the supported portion and list what the transcript does not establish.

## Not answerable

Say that the video does not provide enough evidence. Do not fill the gap from memory unless the user explicitly asks for external research; label external facts separately.

## Multiple videos

Use `(Video title or ID, HH:MM:SS)` for each claim. Distinguish agreement, disagreement, and independent coverage.

## Generated captions

Treat names, numbers, code, and technical terms as error-prone. Cross-check nearby segments or on-screen/source material when available.
