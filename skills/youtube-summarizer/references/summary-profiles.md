# Summary profiles

## Brief

- 3–5 sentence overview
- 5 key takeaways
- 3–6 timestamps

## Standard

- Thesis
- Key points
- Chapter outline
- Examples
- Action items
- Caveats

## Study notes

- Learning objectives
- Definitions
- Topic-by-topic notes
- Worked examples
- Revision questions
- Glossary

## Executive

- Decision relevance
- Claims and evidence
- Risks and assumptions
- Recommended follow-ups

## Content repurposing

- Hooks
- Quotable moments
- Candidate clips with start/end
- Social post angles
- Do not present predicted performance as fact
