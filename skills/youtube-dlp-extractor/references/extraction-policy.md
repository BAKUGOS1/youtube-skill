# Extraction policy

Proceed with downloads only when the user states or reasonably establishes that the content is:

- their own upload;
- licensed for the intended use;
- public-domain or permissively licensed;
- supplied with creator/platform permission; or
- otherwise legally authorized.

Metadata inspection may proceed without downloading media.

Do not help bypass:

- DRM or encrypted streams;
- subscription, membership, rental, or purchase restrictions;
- private/unlisted access controls without authorization;
- geographic or age restrictions;
- platform enforcement or copyright detection.

If rights are unclear, offer metadata/transcript analysis and ask the user to provide an authorized local file.
